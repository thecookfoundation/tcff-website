Our Story

After winning the lottery in 2022, Thomas Cook chose to honor the values of family and community. Together with his friend Joe, he established The Cook & Family Foundation (TC&FF) to provide meaningful help to students, households, families, and individuals facing hardships that government programs cannot fully address.

We offer direct assistance including cars, temporary shelter, food support, and substantial cash prizes/aid — selected based on genuine need.

Official Rules

  • Must be 18 years or older
  • One application per individual/household
  • Provide truthful information and valid government-issued ID
  • Follow our official X account, like and comment on the latest giveaway post
  • Assistance is awarded based on need and available resources
  • Decisions are final. No purchase necessary
  • Recipients may be asked to share anonymized success stories

Full terms available upon request. We prioritize transparency and fairness.
